I am a 5th-year Ph.D. student in **Electronics Engineering at National Taiwan University (NTU)** and currently a **Visiting Scholar at Purdue University** (Computer Science). 

My research focuses on **Efficient AI** and **Machine Unlearning**. Specifically, I work on:
* **Model Compression:** Quantization and Pruning for Vision Models.
* **Machine Unlearning:** Selective forgetting of certain concepts in pre-trained models.
